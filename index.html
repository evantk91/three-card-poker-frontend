Rules:

  • Place an ante to play and/or a pair plus bet to bet that your hand will be a pair or better
  • You get paid out on the pair plus based on your hand
  • Decide whether to play against the dealer or fold. You should play queens high or better.
  • If you play and beats the dealer, you get paid depending on your hand.
  • If the dealer has a jack high or worse, the play bet is not paid out and is returned.
